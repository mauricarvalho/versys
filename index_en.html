Necessary Documents

Data of the Certification Local Representative

- Corporate Name
- CNPJ
- Full Address
- Email
- Telephone
- Contact Name
The company is entitled not only to the marking of the product in the country after ANATEL´s approval, but it will also have the duty of providing technical support, warranty and parts replacement. In order to require a product´s approval to ANATEL, it requires to have a Brazilian company duly registered and recognized by the Government so that it can act as representative of the manufacturer in the country, when it is a foreign company. Does not have a Local Representative? Don´t worry! We can assist you and recommend a company to you that may help by offering the service of Local Representative.

Manufacturer´s Data

- Corporate Name
- Full Address
- Email
- Telephone
- Contact Name
ANATEL considers "Product Manufacturer" the company that developed the project and is proprietary of the technology of the product that is object of certification.

Manufacturing Facility Data

- Corporate Name
- Full Address
ANATEL considers "Product Manufacturing Facility" the company that performs the final assembly of the product under certification, in the shape as it will be supplied to the market.

User Manual

"User Manual" is a document applicable to all products intended to end/common user. Example: user manual of a cell phone. This document must be presented in the Portuguese language (Brazilian Portuguese).

Product Manual

"Product Manual" is a document applicable to all products that are not intended to end/common user. Example: manual of data network equipment This document must be presented in the Portuguese language (Brazilian Portuguese), English, or Spanish.

Technical Specification

It is a document with basic information regarding the product one seeks to certify:
- Product operating frequency
- Applicable technologies
- Product application
- Installation site
- If the product can be operated by a common user or only by specialized professionals
- Supply of the product (e.g. battery, power supply, car charger, USB, etc.)
To provide a complete and accurate technical specification is essential for a successfully and swiftly certification of your product. Inaccurate technical specifications may generate further non-expected costs, in addition to delays and reworks.
If your product is a cable or a connector, for example, the technical specification can be presented instead of the manual.

External and Internal pictures

External and internal pictures are very important in the certification process. ANATEL, through IG06 V2, requires that both internal and external pictures are high resolution and enables a perfect identification of the product under certification.
External pictures - high resolution pictures in different angles (there should be at least one picture showing the identification of the model name).
Internal pictures - high resolution pictures, front and back, of each plate forming the product without any shield, allowing the display of the entire layout and plates components.

Test Reports

Tests reports will be issued by the laboratory after the performance of the tests. After being issued, documents will be submitted to XXXXX and also to the client.

Electric Scheme

If this document cannot be presented due to confidentiality issues, don´t worry, because we will be able to follow-through with the certification process without its presentation. However, if this document is subsequently requested by ANATEL, it must be presented.

ISO 9001:2015 Certification

ISO 9001:2015 Certificate of the Manufacturing Facility is applicable only in the following situations:
A - For category I products, ISO 9001:2015 Certificate must be presented for each of the manufacturing facility, regardless of the amount.
B - For category II or III products, ISO 9001:2015 Certificate must be presented ONLY when there is MORE THAN ONE manufacturing facility. In this case, ISO 9001:2008 certificates of each of them are presented.

Relevant statements - when applicable

In some situations that may occur in the project there may be a need for some kind of statement.
For instance, when the product submitted to the laboratory was not correctly identified, there is a "statement" to solve this problem.
Another common situation when a statement may be necessary: when the product is too small, is possible to request authorization to ANATEL to only place ANATEL´s number on it (ANATEL: HHHHH-AA-FFFFF) and all other applicable information in the manual
Important: in order to assist you, we have and can provide you with the template of all the letters that may be applicable to your certification process.
If there is only one manufacturing facility for products in these categories, ISO 9001:2015 certificate does not need to be presented.
Important: ISO 9001:2015 Certificate, when applicable and if it is in a foreign language, must always be accompanied of a Portuguese sworn translation.
If you need, we can assist you with the translation service.

Local Representative´s Articles of Incorporation

* Applies only when it is the first approval on behalf of the chosen Representative.
It is important that the "corporate objective" of the agreement contains the description of the activity related to marketing of Telecom and/or electronical products.

Local Representative´s CNPJ Card

* Applies only when it is the first approval on behalf of the chosen Representative.
It is a document that every company duly registered and recognized by the Brazilian Government has. Just inform us about the CNPJ (Tax ID) number of the company that we are able to help you download the CNPJ card from the Federal Revenue website.

Commercial Representation Letter

* In the events when the manufacturer does not have an address in Brazil.
It is a document in which the product manufacturer authorizes the Local Representative to market and also provide technical support, warranties and parts replacement. We have a template of this letter to provide for you.

Warranty Information

It is a form that needs to be filled to inform ANATEL about the warranty period that will be offered to the product, once its marketing begins. We have a template of this form to provide for you.
