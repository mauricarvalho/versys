Documentation required

Local Representative Data

- Corporate Name
- CNPJ Number
- Complete Address
- E-mail
- Phone
- Contact Name
The Local Representative is the company that will be responsible not only for the commercialization of the product in Brazil, but also responsible to provide warranty, technical assistance and spare parts. In order to request a homologation to ANATEL, it is mandatory do have this Brazilian company recognized by the Brazilian Government, so it can represents the manufacturer, when it is a foreigner company.
Do not you have a Local Representative?
Do not worry! We can assist you and recommend a company that can help you with the Local Representative Service.

Manufacturer Data

- Corporate Name
- Complete Address
- E-mail
- Phone
- Contact Name
ANATEL understands the manufacturer as being the “Product Owner”, in other words, the company that developed the product under certification.

Factory Data

- Corporate Name
- Complete Address
ANATEL understands the “Factory” as being the company responsible for the final assembly of the product under certification.

User Manual

The “User Manual” is a document applicable for all products that can be used and/or sold to the final user. Example: User Manual for a Mobile Phone. This document needs to be presented in Portuguese Language (Brazilian Portuguese).

Product Manual

The product manual is a document applicable for all products that are not used and/or sold to the final user. Example: Manual for a Data Network Equipment. This document can be presented in Portuguese (Brazilian Portuguese), English or Spanish Language..

Technical Specification

This document describes the basic technical specifications for the product under certification.It is important to detail the following items:

- Frequency Banda
- Technologies
- Product Application
- Where the Product is installed
- If the product cn be operated by the final user or Just by specialized professionals
- How the product is supplied (Ex: battery, power supply, car charger, USB...etc)
It is very important to provide a complete technical specification in the moment the quote is requested in order to guarantee the agility and precision of your request. Technical Specifications not too precise can generate not only delays, but also reworks and unexpected additional costs.
In case your product is a cable or connector, for example, you can only present this technical specification instead of a manual.

External and Internal pictures

External and Internal pictures and very important for the ANATEL process, as they evidence the product under certification. ANATEL, with the Official Document IG06 V2 requires external and internal pictures in high resolution that allow the perfect identification of the product under certification.
External Pictures – pictures in high resolution and different angles (it is necessary to present at least one Picture showing the identification of the model name)
Internal Pictures – pictures in high resolution, front/back side for each board that compose the product, with no shields. With this, it is possible to visualize the components and layout for all the boards.

Test Reports

The test reports are issued by the Laboratory after all tests are concluded. As soon as they are issued, they are sent to Versys and the Client too.

Electrical Schematics

In case this document cannot be presented due to confidentiality reasons, do not worry because we can proceed with the certification process even in this situation. However, if this document is required by ANATEL later, it should be presented.

ISO 9001 2008 Certificate for the factory

The ISO 9001 2008 Certificate is only applicable in the following situations:
A - For products category I, it is needed to be present the ISO 9001 2008 for each of the factories.
B - For products category II and III, the ISO 9001 2008 Certificate is needed ONLY when there is MORE THAN ONE factory. In this case there are TWO factories or more, it is needed to present the ISO 9001 2008 Certificate for all them.
Important: the ISO 9001 2008, when applicable, always need to be presented with the Sworn translation to Portuguese language.
If you need, we can help you with this translation service.

Declarations - If applicable

In some projects, there may be need some kind of Declaration.
Example: when a sample sent to the laboratory for testing is not properly identified, there is a "Declaration" to be provided by the client in order to solve this problem.
Another common situation in which a Declaration is required: when the product is too small, the client can request authorization to ANATEL in order to placed only the ANATEL number (ANATEL: HHHHH-AA-FFFFF) on the product and all other applicable information in manual
Important: If you need, we can provide you a templates for all Declarations that can be needed for your certification process.

Social Contract from the Local Representative

All Brazilian companies have this kind of document.
In this document it is necessary to have a description related to the “commercialization of telecommunication/electronic equipments”.

CNPJ Card from the Local Representative

All Brazilian companies have this kind of document.
We can contact the Local Representative, if needed, in order to request this document.

Representative Letter *

* It is applicable when the manufacturer does not have an address in Brazil.
It is a document in which the manufacturer authorizes the Local Representative not only to commercialize the product but also to provide technical support, warranty and spare parts. We have a template of this letter to provide you.

Warranty Information

There is a form to be filled in with the minimum warranty information to be provided by the Local Representative. We can provide you the template for this form.
