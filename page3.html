Recomendações para o envio de amostras

• Use Fedex or DHL
• Do NOT declare a price above US$ 3,000.00 in the shipping invoice. If necessary, split the material in different boxes and send them in different days so we do not exceed the 3000 limit.
• Do NOT declare a price that is too low – declare a price close to reality even considering these devices are not for sale.
• Choose the option where the sender will pay for all the taxes (DDP), including the ones that will be generated by the Brazilian government when the samples arrive
• Send to Versys the tracking number once the shipment is done
In case the value is really over than US$ 3000,00, it is recommendable to proceed with a temporary importation. This takes more time and it is not too much cheap, but the client avoid that the material is stuck at the Custom’s Office.
For temporary importations, if you need, I can recommend you some company to assist you.
