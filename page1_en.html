Periodic Maintenance

For a certification maintenance process, there are some factors that need to be assessed to determine if the tests are applicable or if we can do the maintenance only with documentary evidences. These are:
1- If the product underwent changes or not since its last certification;
2 - If ANATEL changed the regulation during the period;
3 - If the product presented some non-compliance in the initial certification and any change for adequacy has become necessary;
4 - If Resolution 529 (Safety Tests) is applicable to the product. If positive, it is required that this test is repeated during maintenance, regardless of the answers from previous items.
Documentation for the product that has not underwent change
• Maintenance Declaration (we provide the template)
• External current pictures of the product
• Internal current pictures of the product
• Tests Reports (the need or not of tests will be confirmed after assessment of 4 items above)
• ISO 9001 Certificate of the Manufacturing Facility - when applicable
Documentation for the product that underwent change
• Maintenance Declaration (we provide the template)
• External current pictures of the product
• Internal current pictures of the product
• Picture of the model identification label
• ISO 9001 Certificate of the Manufacturing Facility - when applicable
• Tests Reports (the need or not of tests will be confirmed after assessment of 4 items initially described)
• Product Manual
• Technical Specification
• Electric Scheme
PS: Some products classified as “category III” may require maintenance too.
