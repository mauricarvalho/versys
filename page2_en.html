Tips to expedite the certification process

- Submit the technical specification of the product containing all the complete information of the product, according to items below. This avoids reworks, delays and unexpected costs in the process:
- Product operating frequency
- Applicable technologies
- Product application
- Installation site
- If the product can be operated by a common user or only by specialized professionals
- Supply of the product (e.g. battery, power supply, car charger, USB, etc.)
- When preparing the samples for submission, assures that they are properly identified.
e.g. If the model of the product under certification is "model X", it is very important that there is a label in the samples or any kind of identification showing this model name described in the product; - When submitting the samples to Laboratory is recommended to specify the number of the process (Versys process number) in the package and that there are instructions for carrying the tests.
This way, the project is quickly identified and we can quickly interact with the laboratory with the instructions, in order also to expedite the performance of the tests; - Refer all documentation of the certification process before the issuance of the tests reports. With that, we can avoid delays and perform a partial assessment of what has been sent to provide you with a feedback on possible necessary corrections;
