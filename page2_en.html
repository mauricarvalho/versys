Tips to speed up the process

- Send the product specification containing the complete information regarding the product, as items below. The complete information avoids rework, unexpected delays and costs in the process:

- Frequency Band
- Applicable Technologies
- Product Application
- Where the product is installed
- If the product can be operated by the final user or only by professionals
- How the product is supplied (Example: battery, power supply, car charger, USB ... etc);
- When preparing the samples for the shipment, make sure they are correctly identified with model name.

Example: If the model name of the product under certification is "Model X", it is very important that the samples have a label or any identification that shows this model name;
- When sending the samples to the Versys it is highly recommended that you describe on the package, the Versys project number and also send instructions for testing.

With this, the project can be identified quickly and with the instructions we can interact with the laboratory in order to speed up the testing;
- Send all documents before the issuance of the test reports. With this, we will avoid delays, as we can have partial evaluations and provide you a feedback confirming if corrections are needed or not.
